## About DeepFin-Intelligence

DeepFin-Intelligence Lab is an open research alliance bridging top-tier academic institutions and financial industry leaders. Our mission is to advance the frontier of Financial AI by translating rigorous academic research into robust, real-world applications.

From establishing rigorous evaluation standards to exploring the deep convergence of artificial intelligence and complex financial workflows, our core research initiatives include:

- Financial Benchmarks & Evaluation: Developing comprehensive, expert-aligned benchmarking frameworks and high-quality datasets to rigorously assess LLM capabilities in real-world finance.

- Financial AI Agents: Building autonomous and multi-agent frameworks for complex financial tasks.

- Deep Financial Research: Leveraging LLMs to automate high-quality, comprehensive industry and company research.

- Quantitative & Time-Series Modeling: Pioneering next-generation financial time-series forecasting and agent-driven alpha factor mining & market timing strategies.

By fostering a collaborative, open-source ecosystem, DeepFin-Intelligence empowers the next generation of financial decision-making and productivity.
